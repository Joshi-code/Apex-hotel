About Apex Hotel

Established in 2019, and built in Maryland Lagos near the state's capital. It is one of the largest hotels in the world. Apex hotel is one of the Bamstar international chain of hotels. The hotel was primarily built to accommodate travellers on government business and religious leaders.

Apex hotel is located in Maryland, near the Lagos state capital. It is close to the Murtala International Airport, Johnson Jakande Tinubu Park and about 11km from the National theater.
